# Requirements Document: ShelfIQ Commerce Copilot

## Introduction

ShelfIQ is an AI Commerce Intelligence Copilot designed to provide actionable insights for retailers and marketplace sellers. The system analyzes competitor pricing, demand trends, seller performance, market signals, promotions, and review sentiment to deliver pricing recommendations, demand forecasts, competitor alerts, listing optimization suggestions, margin risk warnings, and growth actions. The platform serves retail category managers, marketplace sellers, pricing teams, and e-commerce operators who need data-driven decision intelligence to optimize their commerce operations.

## Glossary

- **ShelfIQ_System**: The AI Commerce Intelligence Copilot platform
- **Data_Collector**: Component responsible for gathering market data from external sources
- **Analysis_Engine**: Component that processes collected data and generates insights
- **Alert_Manager**: Component that monitors conditions and triggers notifications
- **Recommendation_Engine**: Component that generates actionable suggestions
- **User**: Any authenticated person using the system (category manager, seller, pricing analyst, or e-commerce operator)
- **Category_Manager**: Retail professional managing product categories
- **Marketplace_Seller**: Individual or business selling products on e-commerce platforms
- **Pricing_Team**: Group responsible for pricing strategy and execution
- **SKU**: Stock Keeping Unit - unique identifier for a product
- **Competitor**: Another seller or retailer offering similar products
- **Market_Signal**: Observable indicator of market conditions or trends
- **Listing**: Product page or entry on an e-commerce platform
- **Margin**: Difference between selling price and cost
- **Demand_Forecast**: Predicted future demand for a product
- **Price_Elasticity**: Measure of demand sensitivity to price changes
- **Review_Sentiment**: Analyzed emotional tone and opinion from customer reviews
- **Promotion**: Temporary price reduction or special offer
- **Dashboard**: User interface displaying key metrics and insights
- **Alert**: Notification triggered by specific market conditions
- **Recommendation**: Suggested action generated by the system

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Data Collection and Integration

**User Story:** As a user, I want the system to automatically collect market data from multiple sources, so that I have comprehensive and current information for decision-making.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Data_Collector runs on schedule, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL retrieve competitor pricing data from configured sources
2. WHEN the Data_Collector runs on schedule, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL retrieve demand trend data from marketplace APIs
3. WHEN the Data_Collector runs on schedule, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL retrieve seller performance metrics from connected platforms
4. WHEN the Data_Collector runs on schedule, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL retrieve promotion data from competitor listings
5. WHEN the Data_Collector runs on schedule, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL retrieve customer review data for sentiment analysis
6. WHEN data collection fails for a source, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL log the error and continue with other sources
7. WHEN new data is collected, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL validate data completeness and format before storage
8. WHEN data is stored, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL timestamp each record with collection time

### Requirement 2: Competitor Price Monitoring

**User Story:** As a pricing team member, I want to monitor competitor pricing in real-time, so that I can respond quickly to market changes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User views a SKU, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL display current competitor prices for equivalent products
2. WHEN competitor prices change by more than a configured threshold, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a price change alert
3. WHEN displaying competitor prices, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show the price history for the past 90 days
4. WHEN multiple competitors sell the same product, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL display the minimum, maximum, and median prices
5. WHEN a competitor launches a promotion, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL flag the promotional pricing separately from regular pricing
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL update competitor price data at least every 4 hours

### Requirement 3: Pricing Recommendations

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want AI-generated pricing recommendations, so that I can optimize revenue and competitiveness.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User requests pricing recommendations for a SKU, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL analyze competitor prices, demand elasticity, and margin targets
2. WHEN generating a pricing recommendation, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL provide a suggested price with expected impact on sales volume
3. WHEN generating a pricing recommendation, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL calculate the expected margin at the recommended price
4. WHEN a recommended price would reduce margin below a configured threshold, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL include a margin risk warning
5. WHEN market conditions change significantly, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL update pricing recommendations within 6 hours
6. WHEN displaying a pricing recommendation, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL explain the key factors influencing the recommendation
7. THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL consider seasonal demand patterns when generating pricing recommendations

### Requirement 4: Demand Forecasting

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want accurate demand forecasts, so that I can plan inventory and pricing strategies effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User requests a demand forecast for a SKU, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL generate predictions for the next 30, 60, and 90 days
2. WHEN generating a demand forecast, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL incorporate historical sales data, seasonal trends, and market signals
3. WHEN generating a demand forecast, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL provide confidence intervals for the predictions
4. WHEN actual demand deviates significantly from forecast, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL recalibrate the forecasting model
5. WHEN displaying a demand forecast, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL visualize historical demand alongside predicted demand
6. WHEN external events impact demand, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL adjust forecasts to reflect the new conditions
7. THE Analysis_Engine SHALL update demand forecasts daily for all active SKUs

### Requirement 5: Market Signal Detection

**User Story:** As a pricing team member, I want to detect important market signals early, so that I can make proactive decisions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Analysis_Engine detects a sustained price trend across competitors, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a market trend alert
2. WHEN the Analysis_Engine detects unusual demand spikes or drops, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a demand anomaly alert
3. WHEN a new competitor enters the market for a tracked SKU, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a new competitor alert
4. WHEN a competitor exits the market for a tracked SKU, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a competitor exit alert
5. WHEN review sentiment shifts significantly for a SKU, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a sentiment change alert
6. WHEN multiple market signals indicate a coordinated event, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL correlate signals and provide integrated insights
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow users to configure alert thresholds and notification preferences

### Requirement 6: Listing Optimization

**User Story:** As a marketplace seller, I want recommendations to optimize my product listings, so that I can improve visibility and conversion rates.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User requests listing optimization for a SKU, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL analyze the current listing content
2. WHEN analyzing a listing, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL compare title, description, and keywords against top-performing competitor listings
3. WHEN generating listing recommendations, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL suggest specific improvements to title, description, images, and keywords
4. WHEN a listing is missing critical information, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL flag the gaps and suggest content
5. WHEN competitor listings change significantly, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL update optimization recommendations within 24 hours
6. WHEN displaying listing recommendations, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL prioritize suggestions by expected impact on visibility
7. THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL analyze review sentiment to identify product strengths to highlight in listings

### Requirement 7: Seller Performance Analytics

**User Story:** As a marketplace seller, I want to track my performance metrics against competitors, so that I can identify improvement opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User views the performance dashboard, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL display key metrics including sales rank, review rating, review count, and fulfillment speed
2. WHEN displaying performance metrics, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show the user's position relative to competitor benchmarks
3. WHEN a performance metric declines below a configured threshold, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a performance warning alert
4. WHEN a competitor improves their performance significantly, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a competitive threat alert
5. WHEN displaying performance trends, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL visualize changes over the past 30, 60, and 90 days
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL calculate a composite performance score based on multiple metrics
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL update performance metrics daily

### Requirement 8: Review Sentiment Analysis

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want to understand customer sentiment from reviews, so that I can identify product issues and opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Analysis_Engine processes customer reviews, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL classify sentiment as positive, neutral, or negative
2. WHEN analyzing reviews, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL extract key themes and topics mentioned by customers
3. WHEN displaying sentiment analysis, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show sentiment distribution and trending topics
4. WHEN negative sentiment increases significantly, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a sentiment risk alert
5. WHEN reviews mention specific product features, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL associate sentiment with those features
6. WHEN comparing products, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL display comparative sentiment analysis across competitors
7. THE Analysis_Engine SHALL process new reviews within 24 hours of publication

### Requirement 9: Margin Risk Management

**User Story:** As a pricing team member, I want to be alerted to margin risks, so that I can protect profitability while remaining competitive.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User configures margin thresholds for a SKU, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL store the minimum acceptable margin percentage
2. WHEN competitor pricing would require a price reduction below margin threshold, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a margin risk alert
3. WHEN displaying pricing recommendations, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL highlight recommendations that maintain margin above threshold
4. WHEN market conditions threaten margins across multiple SKUs, THE Alert_Manager SHALL generate a portfolio margin risk alert
5. WHEN a margin risk alert is generated, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL suggest alternative actions such as cost reduction or product bundling
6. WHEN displaying margin analysis, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show margin trends over time and projected margin at recommended prices
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL calculate margin using user-provided cost data and current or recommended selling prices

### Requirement 10: Growth Action Recommendations

**User Story:** As a marketplace seller, I want actionable growth recommendations, so that I can systematically improve my business performance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User views the growth dashboard, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL display prioritized growth actions
2. WHEN generating growth actions, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL consider pricing optimization, listing improvements, inventory management, and competitive positioning
3. WHEN displaying a growth action, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL include the expected impact, effort required, and implementation steps
4. WHEN a User completes a growth action, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL track the actual impact against the predicted impact
5. WHEN growth actions are completed, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL learn from outcomes to improve future recommendations
6. WHEN multiple growth opportunities exist, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL rank them by expected return on effort
7. THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL generate new growth action recommendations weekly

### Requirement 11: Competitive Intelligence Dashboard

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want a comprehensive dashboard of competitive intelligence, so that I can quickly assess market position and opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User accesses the dashboard, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL display key metrics including market share, price position, and performance ranking
2. WHEN displaying the dashboard, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show active alerts and their priority levels
3. WHEN displaying the dashboard, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show recent market changes and trending signals
4. WHEN a User selects a time period, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL update all dashboard visualizations to reflect the selected period
5. WHEN a User selects a specific SKU or category, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL filter dashboard data to the selected scope
6. WHEN critical alerts exist, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL highlight them prominently on the dashboard
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow users to customize dashboard layout and visible metrics

### Requirement 12: Alert and Notification System

**User Story:** As a user, I want to receive timely alerts about important market changes, so that I can respond quickly to opportunities and threats.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an alert condition is met, THE Alert_Manager SHALL create an alert record with timestamp, type, severity, and details
2. WHEN an alert is created, THE Alert_Manager SHALL deliver notifications through configured channels including email, SMS, and in-app
3. WHEN a User configures notification preferences, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL respect channel preferences and quiet hours
4. WHEN multiple related alerts occur, THE Alert_Manager SHALL group them into a single notification to avoid alert fatigue
5. WHEN a User acknowledges an alert, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL mark it as read and remove it from active alerts
6. WHEN an alert condition resolves, THE Alert_Manager SHALL automatically close the alert and notify the user
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain an alert history for audit and analysis purposes

### Requirement 13: Data Export and Reporting

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want to export data and generate reports, so that I can share insights with stakeholders and integrate with other systems.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User requests a data export, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL generate files in CSV, Excel, or JSON format
2. WHEN exporting data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL include all visible columns and applied filters
3. WHEN a User schedules a report, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL generate and deliver the report automatically at the specified frequency
4. WHEN generating a report, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL include visualizations, key metrics, and narrative insights
5. WHEN a report is generated, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL deliver it through configured channels including email and cloud storage
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL provide API endpoints for programmatic data access
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain data export logs for compliance and audit purposes

### Requirement 14: User Authentication and Authorization

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want secure user authentication and role-based access control, so that sensitive business data is protected.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User attempts to log in, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL authenticate credentials against the user database
2. WHEN authentication succeeds, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL create a secure session with expiration
3. WHEN a User accesses a resource, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL verify the user has appropriate permissions
4. WHEN a User belongs to multiple organizations, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow switching between organization contexts
5. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL support role-based access control with roles including Admin, Manager, Analyst, and Viewer
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL enforce password complexity requirements and support multi-factor authentication
7. WHEN suspicious login activity is detected, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL require additional verification

### Requirement 15: System Configuration and Customization

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want to configure system behavior and customize settings, so that the platform meets our specific business needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an Admin configures data collection schedules, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL update the Data_Collector to run at specified intervals
2. WHEN an Admin configures alert thresholds, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL apply the new thresholds to future alert evaluations
3. WHEN an Admin configures competitor tracking, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL add or remove competitors from monitoring
4. WHEN an Admin configures cost data for SKUs, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL use the cost data for margin calculations
5. WHEN an Admin configures marketplace integrations, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL authenticate and establish connections to external platforms
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL validate configuration changes before applying them
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain an audit log of all configuration changes

### Requirement 16: Performance and Scalability

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want the platform to handle large data volumes and concurrent users efficiently, so that performance remains acceptable as usage grows.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the ShelfIQ_System processes data collection, THE system SHALL handle at least 100,000 SKUs per organization
2. WHEN multiple Users access the system concurrently, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL support at least 500 concurrent sessions
3. WHEN a User requests a dashboard or report, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL respond within 3 seconds for 95% of requests
4. WHEN the Analysis_Engine processes data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL complete analysis for all SKUs within 1 hour of data collection
5. WHEN the database grows, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain query performance through indexing and optimization
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL implement caching for frequently accessed data to reduce database load
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL monitor system performance and alert administrators when thresholds are exceeded

### Requirement 17: Data Quality and Validation

**User Story:** As a user, I want confidence in data accuracy, so that I can trust the insights and recommendations provided by the system.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN data is collected, THE Data_Collector SHALL validate data types, ranges, and required fields
2. WHEN invalid data is detected, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL log the validation error and exclude the data from analysis
3. WHEN data quality issues are detected, THE Alert_Manager SHALL notify administrators of data quality problems
4. WHEN displaying data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL indicate the freshness of data with last-updated timestamps
5. WHEN data sources are unavailable, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL use the most recent valid data and indicate staleness
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL implement data deduplication to prevent duplicate records
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain data lineage to track the source and transformations of each data point

### Requirement 18: Historical Data and Trend Analysis

**User Story:** As a category manager, I want access to historical data and trend analysis, so that I can understand long-term patterns and make informed strategic decisions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User views historical data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL provide access to at least 12 months of historical records
2. WHEN displaying trends, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL calculate and visualize moving averages, growth rates, and seasonality patterns
3. WHEN comparing time periods, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show year-over-year and period-over-period changes
4. WHEN analyzing trends, THE Analysis_Engine SHALL identify statistically significant changes and patterns
5. WHEN displaying historical data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow users to select custom date ranges
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL archive historical data older than 24 months to optimize performance while maintaining accessibility
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL support exporting historical data for external analysis

### Requirement 19: Multi-Marketplace Support

**User Story:** As a marketplace seller, I want to manage products across multiple marketplaces from a single platform, so that I can efficiently operate my multi-channel business.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a User connects multiple marketplace accounts, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL collect data from all connected marketplaces
2. WHEN displaying data, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow filtering and grouping by marketplace
3. WHEN generating recommendations, THE Recommendation_Engine SHALL consider marketplace-specific factors such as fees and policies
4. WHEN comparing performance, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL show metrics across all marketplaces in a unified view
5. WHEN a SKU exists on multiple marketplaces, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL track and display performance separately for each marketplace
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL support major marketplaces including Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and Shopify
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL allow users to configure marketplace-specific settings and preferences

### Requirement 20: API and Integration Capabilities

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want robust API and integration capabilities, so that ShelfIQ can connect with our existing business systems.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an external system requests data via API, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL authenticate the request using API keys or OAuth tokens
2. WHEN an API request is received, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL validate request parameters and return appropriate error messages for invalid requests
3. WHEN an API request is successful, THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL return data in JSON format with appropriate HTTP status codes
4. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL provide API endpoints for retrieving pricing data, recommendations, alerts, and performance metrics
5. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL provide webhooks for real-time notification of alerts and significant events
6. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL implement rate limiting to prevent API abuse
7. THE ShelfIQ_System SHALL maintain comprehensive API documentation with examples and authentication instructions
